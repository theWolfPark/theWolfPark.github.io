Dice Game

GAME RULES:
*Enter the winning score.
*The default winning score is 100
*Roll the dice.
*If you roll a 1, you score nothing
and it is now the next player's turn.
*If you roll any other number, it is
added to your turn total and your
turn continues.
*If you choose to "hold",your turn
total is added to your score, and it is
now the other player's turn.
*The first player to the set score wins!!
*Click new game to start a new game.
